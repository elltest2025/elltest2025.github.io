Welcome to ELL

The Econometrics Learning Lab (ELL) at Indiana University is dedicated to developing and implementing innovative methodologies for analyzing large-dimensional economic data.

In today's data-rich environment, we focus on creating tools that effectively handle complex datasets in economic applications. Our lab equips faculty and graduate students with essential skills for utilizing machine learning in economic analysis.

Recent Achievements

Inaugural Workshop Success

Our Machine Learning in Economics workshop featured comprehensive coverage of:

  • Large dimensional regression techniques (PCA, Ridge, Lasso)
  • Reproducing kernel Hilbert space and kernel methods
  • Support vector machines with linear and kernel tricks
  • Tree-based approaches and neural networks
