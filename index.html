About the ELL

The Econometric Learning Lab (ELL) is dedicated to exploring cutting-edge techniques in econometrics. Our mission is to foster collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing among researchers, students, and professionals from various fields.

We host presentations, workshops, and lectures to engage the academic community and further understanding of quantitative methods. Join us to learn, collaborate, and contribute to the future of econometrics.
