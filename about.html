About ELL

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

The Econometric Learning Lab (ELL) aims to be a leading research center in developing and implementing innovative methodologies for analyzing large-dimensional economic data.

We strive to bridge the gap between theoretical econometrics and practical applications in the modern data-rich environment. Our focus is on creating tools and methodologies that can effectively handle the complex datasets encountered in contemporary economic research.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop and disseminate innovative methodologies and econometric theories specifically designed for analyzing large-dimensional economic data. We aim to equip faculty and graduate students with the necessary skills and knowledge to utilize machine learning tools for economic data analysis.

Strategic Goals

Research Innovation

Develop cutting-edge methodologies for analyzing complex economic data sets

Education & Training

Provide comprehensive training and support for faculty and students in advanced econometric methods

Collaboration

Foster collaborative research environments and partnerships across institutions

Field Advancement

Drive the field of econometrics forward through innovative theories and applications

Research Focus Areas

Large-Scale Data Analysis

Developing methods to handle and analyze large-dimensional economic datasets effectively

Machine Learning Applications

Integrating modern ML techniques with traditional econometric approaches

Time Series Analysis

Advanced methods for analyzing temporal economic data
