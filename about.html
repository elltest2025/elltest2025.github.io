About ELL

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

The Econometric Learning Lab (ELL) aims to be a leading research center in developing and implementing innovative methodologies for analyzing large-dimensional economic data. We strive to bridge the gap between theoretical econometrics and practical applications in the modern data-rich environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop and disseminate innovative methodologies and econometric theories specifically designed for analyzing large-dimensional economic data. We aim to equip faculty and graduate students with the necessary skills and knowledge to utilize machine learning tools for economic data analysis.

Strategic Goals

Research Innovation

Develop cutting-edge methodologies for economic data analysis

Education

Provide comprehensive training and support for faculty and students

Collaboration

Foster collaborative research environments and partnerships

Field Advancement

Drive the field of econometrics forward through new theories and applications

Our Supporters

The ELL is generously supported by the Department of Economics at Indiana University and various research grants. Our supporters enable us to continue our mission of advancing economic research through innovative methodologies.
