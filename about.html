About ELL

Vision and Mission of the Econometric Learning Lab

Vision

The Econometric Learning Lab (ELL) aims to be a leading research center in developing and implementing innovative methodologies for analyzing large-dimensional economic data. We strive to bridge the gap between theoretical econometrics and practical applications in the modern data-rich environment.

Our Supporters

The ELL is generously supported by the Department of Economics at Indiana University and various research grants. Our supporters enable us to continue our mission of advancing economic research through innovative methodologies.
